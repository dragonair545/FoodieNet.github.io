Butter Chicken is a popular Indian dish consisting of chicken in a buttery sauce.

It is very popular everywhere and originates from Delhi when leftover tandoori chicken

was mixed with in a butter-rich tomato gravy. Butter chicken also has many variations.

For example, there is such a thing as butter chicken pizza! Not only that, it is used as fillings in:

poutine, wraps, roti, and rolls, along with pie!

Measurements for cooking:

Ingredient Quantity
Oil 1 tbsp
Butter 1 tbsp
Onion(Diced) 1 medium one
Ginger 1 teaspoon(Minced)
Garlic 2 cloves(Crushed)
Chicken Breasts 3 pieces(Cut into 3/4 inch chunks)
Tomato Paste 4 tbsp (1 8oz can)
Garam Masala 1 tbsp
Chili powder 1 teaspoon
Fenugreek 1 teaspoon
Cumin 1 teaspoon
Salt 1 teaspoon
Black Pepper 1/4 teaspoon
Heavy Cream 1 cup

1.Heat a large skillet or medium saucepan over medium-high heat. Add the oil, butter, and onions and cook onions down until lightly golden, about 3-4 minutes. Add ginger and garlic and let it cook for 30 seconds, stirring so it doesn't burn.

2.Add the chicken, tomato paste, and spices. Cook for 5-6 minutes or until everything is cooked through.

3.Add the heavy cream and simmer for 8-10 minutes stirring occasionally. Serve over Basmati rice or with naan.
